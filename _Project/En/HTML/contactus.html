Common Questions

Where can I get more information?
  • Contact customer service
  • Download Tuwaiq Bus App
  • Through the Tuwaiq bus site
  • Help button on ticket vending machines (available at bus stops)
  • Tuwaiq bus staff at the bus stations
What is the ticket price?
  • Two-hour ticket for 4 riyals*
  • 3-day ticket for 20 riyals*
  • Ticket for 7 days at a price of 40 riyals*
  • 30 days ticket priced at 140 riyals*
  • Children up to six years old travel for free
How do I pay the fare for the trip by bus?
  • You can buy a one-time "Darb" card of 10 Saudi riyals, which can be reused as it is an electronic wallet, and it can be purchased through ticket vending machines at bus stations or ticket offices, and it can be recharged from the same machines.
  • By using the (Mada) bank card or credit cards and smart devices equipped with the NFC feature, by passing it through the ticket reader inside the bus.
  • Buy the ticket through the Tuwaiq Bus application
Can I eat or drink on the bus?
  • For the comfort and safety of passengers and for the cleanliness of the buses, eating and drinking is not allowed on the buses, and any food or drink you bring on board must be in a closed, spill-proof container.
Is there a family section on the bus?
  • Yes, there are designated seats for families at the front of the bus.
What should I do if something is lost on the bus?
  • Contact customer service or by sending a message in the lost and found field on the contact us page on the Tuwaiq bus application or website.
Is it possible to enter the bus using a wheelchair?
  • Yes, the buses are equipped with ramps for wheelchairs, and spaces to accommodate all requirements for people with special needs.
Who is eligible for the discounted card?
  • Discounted cards are available for those under the age of 18, students, people with disabilities, cancer patients, first-degree martyrs, and those aged 60 years and over. Holders of discounted cards are entitled to a 50% discount on their ticket price..
