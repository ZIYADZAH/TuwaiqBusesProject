About Tuwaiq buses

The Royal Commission launched the "Tuwaiq Buses" service, as part of the King Abdulaziz Public Transport Project in the city Riyadh, where the launch includes 5 phases to reach the full operation of the service, and the first phase was launched in the month of Sha'ban From this year (March 2023 AD) and the second stage in the month of Dhu al-Hijjah (corresponding to June 2023 AD) to serve passengers in many From the districts of Riyadh, bringing the total number of buses to 560 buses serving 24 lanes and covering more than 1,100 stations and stopping points

The total length of the "Riyadh Buses" network, after the operation of the five phases, is 1900 km, and the total number of buses There will be more than 800 buses, and they will operate across 86 lanes, serving passengers through more than 2,900 stations and stopping points. The service will contribute to Allowing ease of movement for all segments of society through the use of buses as a means of reaching workplaces and education and recreational activities, reduce the need to use the private car, and thus reduce traffic congestion in Riyadh.

In conjunction with the launch of the "Riyadh Buses" service, the electronic portal was launched, which offers options A variety of services for passengers to know the bus routes, and the mechanism for purchasing tickets Many services, including trip planning from the option to choose your destination, tracking the trip through the interactive map and purchasing the tickets.

The cost of a ticket for the trip aboard "Tuwaiq Buses" is 4 riyals, valid for two hours, starting from the first login on the website One of the buses or by activating the ticket through the application, and it is possible to transfer to another bus with the same ticket during this period Duration, and there are additional options for tickets that give passengers multiple options, including 3 days, 7 days and 30 days. The ticket purchase mechanism has also been facilitated to benefit from the service, to keep pace with the needs of all passengers, by making several available Options, including: "Darb Card" or through the application, or vending machines at bus stations, or by paying a value The ticket on the bus using bank cards and smart devices. It should be noted that children up to the age of six years their trip is free.

Tuwaiq Buses will contribute to preserving the environment and public health in the city, by operating environmentally friendly buses It operates using "low sulfur" fuel, as well as contributing to the reduction of air pollution resulting from CO2 emissions The carbon generated from vehicle exhausts, by reducing reliance on the use of private cars, with the availability of a means of transportation General safe, convenient and easy.

It is noteworthy that the King Abdulaziz project for public transport in Riyadh includes the implementation of an integrated network of trains and buses, It works to meet the existing and expected future mobility requirements in the city of Riyadh, and provides a modern and safe transportation service In various parts of the capital, it provides smart city solutions to its residents and visitors, thus contributing to its development and improvement of its environment Urban area.
